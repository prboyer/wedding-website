How We Met

Haley and Paul don’t recall exactly when they first met. A sophomore at UW–Madison, Haley and her friends would often head to the Pink Palace (the nickname given to her friend's brother's apartment building) on the weekends. They would hang out, jam to music, and play games with her friend's brother and his friends, who were seniors at UW. Little did Haley know that in this group was her future husband, Paul.

Gradually, Haley and Paul started to notice each other more and more. Haley's brother had already been friends with Paul for a few years, so Haley would use her brother as an excuse to strike up a conversation with Paul.

At the 2019 New Year's Eve party, Haley and Paul sat by each other and talked the whole evening. The clock struck midnight and ... they didn't kiss! They were both too nervous to make a move!

Not too long after, Paul found the courage to admit that he had feelings for Haley. They finally had their first date on January 15, 2019.

A true gentleman, Paul asked Haley where she wanted to go for their date, and she thoughtlessly suggested McDonald's. In hindsight, Haley is glad that they didn't have their first date at McDonald's. She's not sure why she suggested that in the first place—she doesn't even like McDonald's!

Thanks to Paul, they ended up at a cute little place called Indie Coffee. Haley ordered a fancy vanilla latte, and Paul, to her confusion, ordered a boring black coffee.

The two sat down at a table, started chatting, and began to fall in love.

The Proposal

The date was Friday, December 16, 2022.

Paul and Haley were enjoying themselves at the company Christmas party. Among the festivities and end-of-year presentations, there was a trivia question that asked about the theme of the current State Capitol Christmas tree (Waters of Wisconsin, by the way). This fact will be important to remember later on!

As the party started to wind down, Paul was anxious to get going, and Haley was in agreement. She was recovering from the stomach flu and wanted nothing more than to go home and take a nice long nap.

Paul and Haley got into the truck and started to drive home. So far, nothing was out of the ordinary. Haley started talking about work and how she had enjoyed the party. While mid-conversation, Haley noticed Paul wasn’t driving in the direction of their apartment. When asked, “Where are we going?” Paul replied, "That trivia question got me thinking⁠—we should go and see the Capitol Christmas tree.” Haley reluctantly agreed. Although she wanted to go home, she knew it would only be a quick stop. The two arrived at the Capitol Square and walked into the ornate building. Haley noticed that Paul could not keep his fingers still as they were holding hands.

When they got inside, Paul took Haley up to the second-level balcony to gaze at the beautiful, twinkly tree. "Do you know what today is?" Paul asked. "It's exactly one month away from our four-year anniversary." Paul proceeded to tell Haley how much he loved her, and that he couldn't imagine spending the rest of his life with anyone else. He got down on one knee and pulled out the most beautiful ring that Haley had ever seen. She was so excited that she said yes three times! Before Paul had a chance to place it on her finger, Haley plucked the ring right out of its box and put it on herself!

Paul had arranged for their mutual friend to hide out on the third-floor balcony and record the entire proposal. He also admitted to Haley that he hadn't a clue that the Christmas party was going to feature that trivia question about the Christmas tree⁠—it was a perfect coincidence!

Haley and Paul went out for celebratory champagne and dinner to follow the big event. So much for a quick stop!
